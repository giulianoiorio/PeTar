Estimate the final mass after CE coalescence using a simple precription\+: Mf = (Mc1 + Mc2) + K\+\_\+\+N\+CE $\ast$ (M2\+\_\+\+N\+CE) + k\+\_\+\+CE $\ast$ (M1\+\_\+\+CE + M2\+\_\+\+CE), where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}

\item K\+\_\+\+N\+CE\+: fraction \mbox{[}0,1\mbox{]} of the (non core) mass of the secondary M2\+\_\+\+N\+CE not participating to the CE (e.\+g. MS) reteained after the CE
\item K\+\_\+\+CE\+: fraction \mbox{[}0,1\mbox{]} of the (non core) mass of the primary M1\+\_\+\+CE and secondary M2\+\_\+\+CE participating to the CE reteained after the CE
